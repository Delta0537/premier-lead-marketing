## Fonts to Use in Canva

### For Names & Headlines
- **Font:** Inter
- **Weight:** Bold (700)
- **Size:** 18-24px
- **Color:** #1e293b (light bg) or #ffffff (dark bg)

### For Titles
- **Font:** Inter
- **Weight:** Medium (500)
- **Size:** 13-14px
- **Color:** #3b82f6

### For Contact Info
- **Font:** Inter
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Size:** 12px
- **Color:** #64748b

### For Taglines/Captions
- **Font:** Inter
- **Weight:** Regular (400) Italic
- **Size:** 10-11px
- **Color:** #94a3b8

### For "PLM" Logo Text (if recreating)
- **Font:** Orbitron (or Audiowide as fallback)
- **Weight:** Bold
- **Color:** #3b82f6

## Canva Quick Setup Steps

### 1. Create Brand Kit
1. Open Canva → Brand Hub → Brand Kit
2. Add colors listed above
3. Upload logos from `/logos/` folder
4. Add fonts (Inter, Orbitron)

### 2. Create Email Signature Template
1. Custom size: 600 x 200 px
2. Add elements from Brand Kit
3. Save as template for team use

### 3. Export Settings
- Format: PNG
- Quality: High
- Background: Transparent (optional)
