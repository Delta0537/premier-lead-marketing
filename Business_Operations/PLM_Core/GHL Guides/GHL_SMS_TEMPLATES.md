## SMS BEST PRACTICES

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Keep it under 160 characters when possible
- Use first names
- Sign your texts ("- Andrew PLM")
- Be conversational and friendly
- Include clear call-to-action
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Respect time zones (no texts before 8am or after 8pm)

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Send too frequently (max 2-3 per week)
- Use all caps (LOOKS LIKE SHOUTING)
- Send long paragraphs (use email instead)
- Be overly salesy
- Forget to identify yourself
- Send generic messages
- Text after hours without permission

## SMS AUTOMATION WORKFLOWS

### Workflow 1: New Lead Sequence
**Trigger:** Form submission

1. **Immediate:** Auto-response SMS (Template #1)
2. **Within 1 hour:** Personal call/text

## SETTING UP IN GHL - STEP BY STEP

### Step 1: Create SMS Template
1. Go to **Marketing** ‚Üí **Templates** ‚Üí **SMS**
2. Click **+ Create Template**
3. Name template (e.g., "PLM - SMS Auto Response")

### Step 2: Write Message
1. Type or paste message
2. Add merge fields: `{{contact.first_name}}`
3. Check character count (bottom of screen)
4. Preview with test data

### Step 3: Add to Workflows
1. Go to **Automation** ‚Üí **Workflows**
2. Find appropriate workflow
3. Add action: "Send SMS"
4. Select your template
5. Set timing/delays

### Step 4: Compliance Settings
1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Business Profile** ‚Üí **SMS Settings**
2. Enable "Include unsubscribe link" (if required in your area)
3. Set business hours for SMS sending
4. Add compliance footer if needed

## SMS COMPLIANCE & BEST PRACTICES

### TCPA Compliance (US)
- ‚úÖ Get explicit consent before texting (checkbox on forms)
- ‚úÖ Include opt-out instructions
- ‚úÖ Honor opt-outs immediately
- ‚úÖ Don't text before 8am or after 9pm (local time)
- ‚úÖ Identify your business clearly
