### Step 2: Design Email
1. Choose blank template or drag-drop builder
2. Paste email content above
3. Format text (headings, bold, colors)
4. Add merge fields: `{{contact.first_name}}`
5. Add branding (logo, signature, colors)

### Step 3: Use Merge Fields
Common merge fields:
- `{{contact.first_name}}`
- `{{contact.last_name}}`
- `{{contact.company}}`
- `{{contact.phone}}`
- `{{contact.email}}`
- `{{appointment.start_time}}`
- `{{opportunity.value}}`

### Step 4: Add to Workflows
