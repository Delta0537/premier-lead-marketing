TikTok Marketing Tips

Best Posting Times: Post between 6-10 AM and 7-9 PM for maximum engagement. Test different times to find what works for your audience.
Hashtag Strategy: Use 3-5 relevant hashtags like #LeadGeneration #AIMarketing #BusinessGrowth #DigitalMarketing #Entrepreneur
Video Content: Create short videos (15-30 seconds) showing before/after results, client testimonials, or quick tips about lead generation.
Call-to-Action: Always include a clear CTA directing viewers to visit premierleadmarketing.ai or comment for more info.
A/B Testing: Run all three variations simultaneously to see which performs best with your target audience.
